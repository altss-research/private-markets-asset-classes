# Venture Capital

Venture Capital refers to equity investments in early-stage and high-growth private companies, typically focused on innovation-driven sectors.

- Minority ownership positions

- Long investment horizon
- Emphasis on company growth over near-term cash flow
- Active involvement through boards, advisory roles, and strategic support

## Common Structures

- Closed-end venture funds
- Multi-stage funds
- Opportunity or crossover funds
- Sector-focused or thematic funds

## Common Allocator Use Cases

- Access to early-stage innovation
- Long-term growth exposure
- Portfolio diversification across maturity stages
