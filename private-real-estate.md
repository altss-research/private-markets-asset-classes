# Private Real Estate

Private Real Estate refers to investments in income-producing or value-enhancement real property executed through non-public vehicles. These strategies focus on acquiring, developing, repositioning, or operating real estate assets across property types and geographies.

## Characteristics

- Backed by tangible real property assets  
- Returns driven by rental income, asset appreciation, and operational execution  
- Exposure to local market dynamics such as supply, demand, and demographics  
- Long-duration investment horizon with limited liquidity  
- Sensitivity to interest rates, financing conditions, and macroeconomic cycles  

## Common Structures

- Closed-end private real estate funds  
- Open-ended and evergreen real estate vehicles  
- Asset-level joint ventures and programmatic partnerships  
- Separate accounts and co-investment structures  

## Common Allocator Use Cases

- Income generation  
- Inflation protection through rent escalation  
- Portfolio diversification  
- Long-term capital appreciation
