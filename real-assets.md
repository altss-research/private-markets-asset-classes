# Real Assets

Real Assets refer to investments in physical or tangible assets that generate returns through income, inflation linkage, or long-term appreciation.

## Characteristics

- Backed by physical or hard assets  
- Cash flows derived from leases, usage, or production  
- Inflation sensitivity or inflation protection features  
- Long-duration investment horizon  
- Typically lower correlation with public equity markets  

## Common Structures

- Core, core-plus, value-add, and opportunistic funds  
- Closed-end and open-ended structures  
- Asset-level joint ventures  
- Programmatic or portfolio-based vehicles  

## Common Allocator Use Cases

- Inflation hedging  
- Income generation  
- Portfolio diversification  
- Capital preservation over long horizons
